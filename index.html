ABOUT KYRGYZSTAN

The Kyrgyz Republic is located in the north-eastern part of Central Asia, occupies the western part of the Tien Shan mountain system and the northern mountainous regions of Pamir-Alai. More than 80% of the territory of the republic is located at an altitude of more than 1500 meters above sea level.

Spend your summer at Issyk-Kul!

Issyk-Kul with its crystal clear water, mineral springs, magnificent sandy beaches and combination of sea and mountain climate is an excellent resort area with mud and thermo-mineral treatment.

Skiing in Karakol

Very close to Karakol you will find beautiful mountain landscapes with snowy peaks and spectacular views of waterfalls, lakes and canyons with red rocks.
